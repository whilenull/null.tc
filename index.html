how we work

Simpler is better

While designing complex solutions can be fun, we want to produce impactful and efficient results by simplifying problems.

People matter

Development is more than writing code. Long lasting results and meaningful work cannot happen without humans collaborating. Being excellent to each other is a skill that no amount of technical knowledge can replace.

Sharing knowledge

Knowledge is a tool, not an end. It does not define us. We should liberate ourselves from having all the answers and retaining what we know. We can then focus on creating real value.
